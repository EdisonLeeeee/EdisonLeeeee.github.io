## About me

I am currently a PhD student at [Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU)](https://www.sysu.edu.cn/), where I am advised by Prof. [Liang Chen](https://chenliang.tech/). I received the master's degree from Sun Yat-sen University in 2021. My research interests are in the area of **Trustworthy Graph Learning**. More specifically, I work on the area of graph neural networks including their theory foundations, reliability and applications.
