About me

I am currently a Ph.D student at Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU), where I am advised by Prof. Liang Chen. I received the master's degree from Sun Yat-sen University in 2021. My research interests are in the area of Trustworthy Graph Learning. More specifically, I work on the area of graph neural networks including their theory foundations, reliability and applications.

My research interests include:

  • Trustworthy Graph Learning

  • Graph Neural Networks

  • Spiking Neural Networks
